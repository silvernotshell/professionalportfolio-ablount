About Me

My name is Annagrace Blount and I am an aspirant web developer. I am 24 years old and I graduated from The University of North Carolina at Charlotte in May of 2023, receiving a Bachelor's degree in Communications with a concentration in Public Advocacy. I chose this path for collegiate education because I wanted to learn how to become an efficient communicator and a proficient advocate. I have four dogs of my own, I foster frequently, and I have dreams of opening my own animal rescue one day.

Following my career as a student, I decided to pursue my dreams of learning how to code. I have always been interested in and skilled with computers, and I have goals of creating applications to aid non-profit organizations across the world. Upon discovering the full stack bootcamp offered by The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, it became evident I could combine something I am passionate about, with something I have had endless curiosity about, to make the world a better place.
